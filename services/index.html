The 7-Layer AEO System

A proven, repeatable framework that transforms how AI assistants see your business. Each layer builds on the previous one to make you the recommendation.

TL;DR

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of making your business the answer when people ask AI assistants for recommendations. The 7-layer system covers answer intent mapping, answer hub creation, brand facts pages, machine-readable data, schema markup, third-party citation building, and AI shopping optimization. Together, these layers make AI assistants discover, verify, and confidently recommend your business. Businesses implementing the full system typically go from 0-5 AI recommendations to 35-45 out of 50 within 90 days.

People are shifting from searching to asking. ChatGPT alone has over 5 billion monthly visits. Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Copilot are growing fast. These AI assistants do not show ten blue links. They give one recommendation. Maybe three. If your brand is not the answer, your competitor is.

AI-referred customers convert at 4.4x the rate of Google organic traffic because they arrive pre-sold. The AI already did the comparison shopping for them. The 7 Layers

Each layer addresses a specific way that AI assistants discover, verify, and recommend businesses. They work together as a system, not in isolation.

1

Answer Intent Mapping

Audit 50+ real questions people ask AI about your category. Map who gets recommended, what sources AI cites, and where your gaps are. This is your competitive intelligence baseline.
2

Answer Hub Creation

Build a dedicated page on your site designed specifically for AI to find, understand, and cite. This single page drives 60%+ of all AI recommendations for a brand.
3

Brand Facts Page

A clean, Wikipedia-style page with every verifiable fact about your business. AI needs to verify facts before recommending you. This makes verification effortless.
4

Machine-Readable Data

A structured JSON file at a standard URL that AI agents can read directly. Clean, structured, trustworthy data served on a silver platter. No scraping needed.
5

Schema Markup Optimization

Structured data that tells AI exactly what your content means. Organization, FAQPage, Product with GTINs, AggregateRating. This also gets you into ChatGPT Shopping results.
6

Third-Party Citation Building

Get your brand mentioned on review sites, comparison pages, Wikidata, Reddit, and Quora. AI trusts external validation. This is what separates occasional mentions from consistent recommendations.
7

AI Shopping Optimization

Optimize product identifiers, titles, images, and reviews for ChatGPT Shopping and Gemini Shopping. Products appear with star ratings and pricing in AI shopping results.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?
Answer Engine Optimization is the practice of making your business the recommended answer when people ask AI assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude for recommendations. Unlike traditional SEO which optimizes for search engine rankings, AEO optimizes for AI recommendations. AI-referred customers convert at 4.4x the rate of organic search traffic because they arrive pre-sold by the AI's recommendation.
How is AEO different from SEO?
SEO gets you on page one of Google's ten blue links. AEO makes you the one recommendation AI gives. SEO focuses on keywords, backlinks, and page authority. AEO focuses on structured data, citation building, answer hubs, and machine-readable content that AI can verify and confidently recommend. AEO layers on top of SEO and the two strategies reinforce each other.
Why does AEO matter for my business?
ChatGPT alone has over 5 billion monthly visits and growing. People are shifting from searching to asking. AI assistants do not show ten options. They recommend one or two. If your brand is not the recommendation, your competitor is. Less than 1% of businesses are currently optimizing for AI recommendations, which means there is a massive first-mover advantage.
How long does AEO take to show results?
Most businesses start appearing in AI recommendations within 60-90 days of implementing the full 7-layer system. The initial build (answer hubs, brand facts, schema markup, machine-readable data) takes 2-4 weeks. Citation building runs in parallel over weeks 5-8. Measurable results typically appear by week 8-12.
Does AEO replace SEO?
No. AEO layers on top of your existing SEO strategy. Your traditional SEO continues to drive Google traffic. AEO adds a completely new, high-converting traffic channel from AI assistants. Strong SEO content actually helps AI models discover and trust your brand, so the two strategies reinforce each other.
Which AI platforms does AEO work for?
The 7-layer AEO system optimizes for all major AI assistants: ChatGPT (including ChatGPT Shopping), Google Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, Microsoft Copilot, and others. Each platform has different data sources and ranking signals, so the system is designed to cover all of them through structured data, citations, and platform-specific optimization.
