Welcome aboard, Travel Taste Discover

Just two quick things and we'll get to work.

1

Create an email alias

Create an email alias like aeo@traveltastediscover.com that forwards to hello@aeothis.com

We use this to set up third-party profiles on your behalf. Every account will belong to your domain, so you keep full ownership.

  1. Log in to your WordPress hosting provider (wherever you manage your @traveltastediscover.com email)
  2. Look for Email Accounts or Email Forwarding in your hosting control panel
  3. Create a new email or alias: aeo@traveltastediscover.com
  4. Set it to forward all mail to hello@aeothis.com
  5. Save your changes
2

Grant us website access

Add us as an admin user on your WordPress site so we can install your AEO pages.

We'll add your Answer Hub, Brand Facts page, and schema markup. This typically takes one session, and you can revoke access anytime after.

  1. Log in to your WordPress Dashboard
  2. Go to Users in the left menu
  3. Click Add New User
  4. Enter hello@aeothis.com as the email
  5. Set the role to Administrator
  6. Click Add New User to send the invite
✓

You're all set!

We'll start work this week and send you your first progress report within 30 days. If you have any questions, reach out anytime at hello@aeothis.com
