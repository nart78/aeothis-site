Welcome aboard, Fit Kids Care!

Just two quick things and we'll get to work.

1

Create an email alias

Create an email alias like aeo@fitkids.ca that forwards to hello@aeothis.com

We use this to set up third-party profiles on your behalf. Every account will belong to your domain, so you keep full ownership.

  1. Go to admin.google.com and sign in with your admin account
  2. Click Directory, then Users
  3. Click on the user you want to add the alias to
  4. Click User information, then Alternate email addresses
  5. Click Add alternate email
  6. Type aeo in the alias field
  7. Click Save
  8. Then set up forwarding: open Gmail for that account, go to Settings > Forwarding, and forward to hello@aeothis.com
2

Grant us website access

Invite us as an admin on your Wix site so we can install your AEO pages.

We'll add your Answer Hub, Brand Facts page, and schema markup. This typically takes one session, and you can revoke access anytime after.

  1. Go to your Wix Dashboard
  2. Click Settings in the left menu
  3. Click Roles & Permissions
  4. Click Invite People
  5. Enter hello@aeothis.com
  6. Set role to Admin
  7. Click Send Invite
✓

You're all set!

We'll start work this week and send you your first progress report within 30 days. If you have any questions, reach out anytime at hello@aeothis.com
