How to Choose the Right AEO Provider

  • Dedicated AEO methodology - Look for a structured system, not general SEO rebranded as "AI optimization." Ask what specific layers or steps they follow.
  • AI platform coverage - The provider should optimize for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Meta AI at minimum. If they only focus on Google AI Overviews, that's traditional SEO.

  • Measurable reporting - You should receive regular reports showing your AI mention rate, recommendation position, and referral traffic from AI platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

Answer Engine Optimization is the practice of optimizing your business and website so that AI assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude recommend you when users ask purchase-intent questions. Unlike SEO which targets search engine rankings, AEO focuses on becoming the direct answer AI gives. It involves structured data optimization, answer hub creation, brand authority building, and third-party citation campaigns.

How much do AEO services cost?

Dedicated AEO services typically range from $300 to $5,000+ per month depending on scope. AEOthis offers plans starting at $300/month (Starter) covering 25 query intent mapping, 1 answer hub, and brand facts setup. Growth plans at $3,000/month include 50+ queries, 3 answer hubs, and citation campaigns. Full-service agencies that include AEO as part of broader digital marketing may charge $5,000-$20,000+/month.

How long does AEO take to show results?

Most businesses start appearing in AI recommendations within 60-90 days of implementing a full AEO strategy. Within 6 months of consistent optimization, AEO can become a significant traffic source with conversion rates 3-4x higher than traditional organic search. The timeline depends on industry competition, existing web presence, and how comprehensively the system is implemented.

What should I look for in an AEO agency?

Look for agencies that cover all major AI platforms, have a structured methodology (not just general SEO rebranded), provide answer intent mapping and monitoring, implement technical AEO (schema markup, brand-facts.json, machine-readable data), and build third-party citations. Ask for case studies showing actual AI recommendation improvements, not just traditional SEO metrics.

Does AEO replace SEO?

No. AEO layers on top of existing SEO. Traditional SEO continues driving Google traffic. AEO adds a new high-converting channel from AI assistants. The two strategies complement each other because strong SEO content helps AI models discover and trust your brand. The best approach is running both simultaneously.

Which AI platforms should AEO target?

A comprehensive AEO strategy should target ChatGPT (5+ billion monthly visits), Google Gemini and AI Overviews (appearing on 60%+ of searches), Meta AI (1+ billion monthly active users), Perplexity (500+ million monthly queries), Claude by Anthropic, and Microsoft Copilot. All platforms prioritize the same trust signals: structured data, authoritative content, and third-party citations.
