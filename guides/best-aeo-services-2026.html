How to Choose an AEO Provider

  • Ask for AI recommendation data, not just SEO metrics. The only metric that matters is: how many target queries is your brand the #1 AI recommendation for? If a provider can't show you this data, they're guessing.
  • Verify they cover all 7 layers. Partial AEO (just schema, or just content) produces partial results. The system works because all layers reinforce each other.
  • Check if they understand answer hub copy. The TL;DR paragraph on your answer hub is the single most important piece of copy for AEO. It needs to be neutral, factual, specific, and written the way you'd want AI to say it. Ask to see examples.
  • Confirm ongoing monitoring is included. AI models update constantly. A one-time AEO build without weekly monitoring will decay. Make sure your provider runs weekly query audits and updates content accordingly.
  • Look for citation building capability. The difference between showing up sometimes and showing up consistently is third-party validation. Your provider should have a process for earning external citations from trusted sources.

AEO Service FAQs

What should I look for in an AEO service provider?
Look for a provider that covers all 7 layers: answer intent mapping, answer hub creation, brand facts pages, machine-readable data, schema markup, third-party citations, and AI shopping optimization. Avoid providers that only focus on one or two layers. Ask for before/after AI recommendation data, not just traditional SEO metrics.
How much does AEO cost?
AEO services typically range from $1,500 to $5,000+ per month depending on scope. A basic implementation covering answer hubs and brand facts starts around $1,500/month. Full-service AEO with citation campaigns and weekly monitoring runs $3,000-5,000/month. Enterprise programs with multi-category optimization are custom priced.
Can I do AEO myself or do I need an agency?
You can implement the core AEO framework yourself if you have content writing skills and basic technical ability for schema markup. The 7-layer system is a proven playbook. An agency brings experience in answer hub copywriting that AI actually cites, technical schema implementation, and established relationships for citation building. Most businesses see faster results with professional help.
Is AEO a one-time project or ongoing?
The initial build (answer hubs, brand facts, schema, machine-readable data) takes 2-4 weeks. But AEO requires ongoing maintenance because AI models update constantly and competitors will catch on. Plan for 90 minutes per week of monitoring and content updates to maintain your recommendation position.
