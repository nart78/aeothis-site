Frequently Asked Questions

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?
Answer Engine Optimization is the practice of optimizing your business and website so that AI assistants like ChatGPT, Meta AI, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude recommend your products or services when users ask buying-intent questions. Unlike traditional SEO which targets search engine rankings, AEO focuses on becoming the direct answer AI gives to users.
How is AEO different from SEO?
SEO optimizes for search engine result pages where you compete as one of 10 blue links. AEO optimizes for AI answer engines where there is typically only one recommendation, maybe three. SEO targets keywords and rankings. AEO targets being the direct, cited answer that AI assistants give to purchase-intent questions. The two strategies complement each other.
How long does it take to see results?
Most businesses start appearing in AI recommendations within 60-90 days of implementing our full AEO system. Within 6 months of consistent optimization, AEO can become your highest-ROI traffic source with conversion rates 3-4x higher than traditional organic search.
Does AEO replace my existing SEO?
No. AEO layers on top of your existing SEO strategy. Your traditional SEO continues to drive Google traffic. AEO adds a completely new, high-converting traffic channel from AI assistants. Strong SEO content actually helps AI models discover and trust your brand, so the two strategies reinforce each other.
Which AI platforms does this work for?
Our AEO system targets all major AI answer engines: ChatGPT (5+ billion monthly visits), Meta AI (1+ billion monthly active users, 22% of the AI search market), Google Gemini and AI Overviews (appearing on 60%+ of searches), Perplexity (500+ million monthly queries), Claude by Anthropic, and Microsoft Copilot. The strategy works across all platforms because they all prioritize the same trust signals: structured data, authoritative citations, and clean factual information.
What kind of businesses benefit from AEO?
Any business where potential customers ask AI for recommendations. E-commerce brands, SaaS companies, professional services, local businesses, health and wellness brands, financial services, B2B companies. If people are asking AI "what is the best [your category]," you need AEO.
How do you measure AEO success?
We monitor your AI recommendation share: how often you appear when customers ask AI platforms about your industry. We run your target queries across ChatGPT, Meta AI, Gemini, and Perplexity regularly and report exactly where you stand versus competitors.
Why is AI referral traffic so valuable?
When an AI tells someone "this is the best [product] for [their need]," they arrive at your site pre-sold. The AI already did the comparison shopping for them. They don't browse 10 tabs or read 20 reviews. They trust the recommendation and act on it. That's why businesses optimized for AI visibility consistently see higher-quality leads from these channels.
