The 7-Layer AEO System

A complete system that makes AI assistants recommend your business. Not theory. A proven, repeatable framework.

1

Answer Intent Mapping

We audit 50+ questions real customers ask AI about your category. We log who gets recommended, what sources AI cites, and where your gaps are. This is your competitive intelligence baseline.

2

Answer Hub Creation

We build a dedicated page on your site designed specifically for AI to find, understand, and cite. Neutral tone, real specs, comparison tables, FAQs, and external citations. This single page drives 60%+ of AI recommendations.

3

Brand Facts Page

A clean, Wikipedia-style page with every verifiable fact about your business. Founded year, certifications, specs, policies, social profiles. AI needs to verify facts before recommending you. We make verification effortless.

4

Machine-Readable Data

We create a structured JSON file at a standard URL on your site that AI agents can read directly. Clean, structured, trustworthy data served on a silver platter. No scraping needed.

5

Schema Markup Optimization

We add the exact structured data that AI models and search engines need: ItemList, FAQPage, Organization, Product with GTINs, AggregateRating. This also gets you into ChatGPT Shopping results.

6

Third-Party Citation Building

AI trusts external validation. We get your brand mentioned on review sites, comparison pages, Wikidata, Reddit, and Quora. Perplexity especially weights third-party citations heavily in its recommendations.

7

AI Shopping Optimization

ChatGPT Shopping pulls from Google Merchant Center. We optimize your product identifiers, titles, attributes, images, and reviews so your products appear with star ratings and pricing in AI shopping results.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?
Answer Engine Optimization is the practice of optimizing your business and website so that AI assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude recommend your products or services when users ask buying-intent questions. Unlike traditional SEO which targets search engine rankings, AEO focuses on becoming the direct answer AI gives to users.
How is AEO different from SEO?
SEO optimizes for search engine result pages where you compete as one of 10 blue links. AEO optimizes for AI answer engines where there is typically only one recommendation, maybe three. SEO targets keywords and rankings. AEO targets being the direct, cited answer that AI assistants give to purchase-intent questions. The two strategies complement each other.
How long does it take to see results?
Most businesses start appearing in AI recommendations within 60-90 days of implementing the full 7-layer system. Within 6 months of consistent optimization, AEO can become your highest-ROI traffic source with conversion rates 3-4x higher than traditional organic search.
Does AEO replace my existing SEO?
No. AEO layers on top of your existing SEO strategy. Your traditional SEO continues to drive Google traffic. AEO adds a completely new, high-converting traffic channel from AI assistants. Strong SEO content actually helps AI models discover and trust your brand, so the two strategies reinforce each other.
Which AI platforms does this work for?
Our AEO system targets all major AI answer engines: ChatGPT (5+ billion monthly visits), Perplexity (500+ million monthly queries), Google Gemini and AI Overviews (appearing on 15%+ of searches), Claude by Anthropic, and Microsoft Copilot. The strategy works across all platforms because they all prioritize the same trust signals: structured data, authoritative citations, and clean factual information.
What kind of businesses benefit from AEO?
Any business where potential customers ask AI for recommendations. E-commerce brands, SaaS companies, professional services, local businesses, health and wellness brands, financial services, B2B companies. If people are asking AI "what is the best [your category]," you need AEO.
How do you measure AEO success?
We track three core KPIs: recommendation share (how many target queries you're #1 for), AI referral traffic volume, and AI referral conversion rate. We run your target queries through ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other platforms weekly and report exactly where you stand versus competitors.
Why is the conversion rate so much higher from AI traffic?
When ChatGPT tells someone "this is the best [product] for [their need]," they arrive at your site pre-sold. The AI already did the comparison shopping for them. They don't browse 10 tabs or read 20 reviews. They trust the recommendation and buy. That's why AI-referred traffic converts at 4.4x the rate of traditional Google organic traffic.
