Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Who We Are

AEOthis is operated by 2191584 Alberta Inc., based in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. We provide Answer Engine Optimization services that help businesses become recommended by AI search platforms.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at our contact form.

Information We Collect

Website visitors

When you visit aeothis.com, we may collect standard web analytics data including pages visited, referring URLs, browser type, and device information. This data is aggregated and not tied to individual identities.

Get Started form and chatbot

When you use our Get Started form or onboarding chatbot, we collect the business information you provide, including:

  • Business name and website URL
  • Contact email address
  • Business category and service area
  • Competitor names
  • Unique selling points
  • Social media links

Client onboarding

When you complete our client onboarding process, we collect:

  • The email alias you create for our use (e.g. aeo@yourdomain.com)
  • Confirmation of website platform access

During service delivery

As part of delivering our AEO services, we may access and process:

  • Your website content and structure
  • Your business information as provided in onboarding
  • Publicly available information about your business (reviews, directory listings, social profiles)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver our AEO optimization services, including creating Answer Hubs, Brand Facts pages, and schema markup on your website
  • Create and manage third-party directory profiles on your behalf, using the email alias you provide
  • Run AI visibility analyses to measure and track your performance
  • Communicate with you about service progress and results
  • Generate proposals and reports for prospective clients
  • Improve our services and website

AI Platform Usage

As part of our analysis process, we query AI platforms (such as Google Gemini and Perplexity) to measure how often your business is recommended. These queries contain your business name and category. We do not send personal contact information, passwords, or login credentials to any AI platform.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our website and deliver our services:

  • GitHub Pages for website hosting
  • Google Fonts for typography
  • Formspree for processing form submissions
  • Resend for transactional email delivery

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Website Access

When you grant us access to your website platform (Shopify, WordPress, Wix, etc.), we use that access solely to implement AEO optimizations as agreed during onboarding. This includes adding pages, installing schema markup, and making content updates. You can revoke our access at any time through your platform's settings.

Data Retention

We retain client data for the duration of our service engagement plus 90 days. After that period, we delete your data from our active systems. Form submissions from prospective clients who do not become clients are deleted upon request.

Content we create on your website (Answer Hubs, Brand Facts pages, schema markup) remains on your website under your control. Third-party profiles created using your email alias belong to you and remain active under your account.

Your Rights

In accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for us to process your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at our contact form. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We do not use advertising networks or retargeting services. Third-party services we embed (such as Google Fonts) may set their own cookies according to their respective policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our website or services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
